Oven Roasted Potatoes

Description

These delicious oven roasted potatoes are very easy to make and can add a rustic element to any meal!

Ingredients

  • Small potatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepepr

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix together oil salt pepper and garlic in mixing bowl
  3. Add potatoes and mix so that they are evenly coated
  4. Bake for 22-24 minutes
