Kentucky Tomato Soup

Description

This is a pretty cool looking bowl of soup. When using the default website provided by The Odin Project, I surprisingly had a lot of trouble saving their image of the Kentucky Tomato Soup. For that reason the photo above is actually a photo of vegetable soup that I retrieved from the internet.

Me personally? Not really super big into soup. In fact I am not entirely sure why this is the entree that I ended up creating first. Seems pretty foolish in hindsight but hey, I am in far too deep to start second guessing it now.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Liquid of some sort
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Chop up your tomatoes
  2. Prepare your mystery liquid of choice
  3. Combine above ingredients as well as seasoning
  4. Boil until it becomes soup
