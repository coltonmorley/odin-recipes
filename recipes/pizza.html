New York Style Pizza

Description

The ingredients and steps below can be used to create a delicious New York style pizza. You can also additionally add and meats or other toppings as you see fit, but cooking times may vary.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Brush dough with olive oil
  3. Add tomato sauce to dough
  4. Add cheese on top
  5. Add pepperonis on top of cheese
  6. Bake for 18 minutes
